All Pets Wellness Foundation

All Pets Wellness Foundation was founded by a group of dedicated animal advocates to provide funds to care for sick and injured pets whose owners cannot afford the treatments.


About All Pets Wellness Foundation

All Pets Wellness Foundation, a recognized 501 c3 organization, was founded to offer companion animal owners an alternative for funding emergency medical care when the financial dictates to support the medical care cannot be met.
